Hello and welcome to a different world of Pokémon!

Normally, pokemon teams are built one by one, member by member. But as the years go by and the Pokédex fills out, this method becomes daunting for newer trainers, and even for trainers simply coming back from hiatus! This application is a tool that Intermediate and Experienced Pokemon trainers can use to build Pokémon teams out of what we here on Smogon call...

Cores: groups of two or three Pokémon who work well together in battle.
