Stall

Pokémon cores in Standard Offense teams focus on pivoting around the opponent’s offense and/or denying enemy support, before breaking down walls enough to ensuring a sweep late in the game. A good Standard Offense team has a 2 or 3 solid strategies to win.

Wall

Pokémon who actively inhibit the opposing team’s winning strategies.

Support

Pokemon who can alter the existing situation to give their teammates an advantage.
